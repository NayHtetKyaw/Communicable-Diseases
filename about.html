    About this projcet

          Digital technology, has taken the important role of every person from the 21st century. As the technology has becoming more advance and advance, it is leading to the people’s daily life style to a more technology oriented and digital co-dependent. Therefore, everything that people want are available on internet. The society itself is based on the internet and digital technologies. The internet can be easily used with every digital device that support wireless connection. So, to reach out to people, newspapers and television are not as effective as then. The most effective way now is by sharing the information into digital society through the internet. By following the flow of the technology advanced, sharing the information from the online, can be reach out to everyone easily. Sharing and helping people learn and improve about the health knowledges from online, applications, etc. is known as the digital well-being. As the researcher, the duty is to investigate that the advanced digital technologies can improve human life or can be distracted. And explore the efficacy of digital products and how to feature specifically and design to improve health and wellbeing. That’s why, the goal is to make a project plan based on the digital wellbeing and develop a project that can help people and their health by using digital technology.
